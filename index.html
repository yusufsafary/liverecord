The Problem LiveRecord Solves

FANS
INVISIBLE.
NO
MORE.

For decades, being a fan meant buying — CDs, records, concert tickets, merchandise. Your devotion was measured by what you could own.

Then the internet changed everything. Social media gave fans a voice. Fandom evolved from passive consumption into active participation — where fans invest not only money, but time, creativity, and emotion.

Yet paradoxically, much of that passion remains invisible in today's entertainment system. Fans create massive value — trending topics, viral content, cultural movements — and receive nothing in return.

Passion, not just capital, should determine worth.

LiveRecord Protocol

FANDOM
ECONOMY
ONCHAIN

LiveRecord Protocol addresses a fundamental problem: fandom powers the entertainment economy, yet fan contributions remain invisible and unrewarded.

Through R.E.C. — Record of Emotional Contribution — the protocol establishes the global engagement index for entertainment. It uses AI to quantify genuine fandom activity across the internet and preserves it permanently on blockchain.

$LRC transforms this into economic value, enabling fans to earn rewards for their devotion while creating permissionless infrastructure for the fandom economy.

01

AI-powered scoring — R.E.C. uses machine learning to detect and quantify genuine fan activity across social platforms, streaming services, and community spaces

Permissionless infrastructure — any IP, any fandom, any community can build on top of LiveRecord Protocol without asking permission
