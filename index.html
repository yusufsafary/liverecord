The Problem LiveRecord Solves

FANS
INVISIBLE.
NO
MORE.

For decades, being a fan meant buying — CDs, records, concert tickets, merchandise. Your devotion was measured by what you could own.

Then the internet changed everything. Social media gave fans a voice. Fandom evolved from passive consumption into active participation — where fans invest not only money, but time, creativity, and emotion.

Yet paradoxically, much of that passion remains invisible in today's entertainment system. Fans create massive value — trending topics, viral content, cultural movements — and receive nothing in return.

Passion, not just capital, should determine worth.

LiveRecord Protocol

FANDOM
ECONOMY
ONCHAIN

LiveRecord Protocol addresses a fundamental problem: fandom powers the entertainment economy, yet fan contributions remain invisible and unrewarded.

Through R.E.C. — Record of Emotional Contribution — the protocol establishes the global engagement index for entertainment. It uses AI to quantify genuine fandom activity across the internet and preserves it permanently on blockchain.

$REC transforms this into economic value, enabling fans to earn rewards for their devotion while creating permissionless infrastructure for the fandom economy.

06 Questions
What is LiveRecord? +
LiveRecord is a blockchain protocol that turns invisible fandom activity into visible, lasting economic value. Using our AI-powered R.E.C. (Record of Emotional Contribution) engine, we score genuine fan engagement across the internet and record it permanently on blockchain. Fans earn $REC token rewards for their devotion.
What is $REC token? +
$REC is the native token of the LiveRecord Protocol and serves as the global fan engagement index for entertainment. It is the benchmark for measuring fandom value onchain. Fans earn $REC by building their R.E.C. Score through genuine fandom activity — streaming, posting, creating fan art, attending events, and more.
How does R.E.C. scoring work? +
R.E.C. (Record of Emotional Contribution) is our AI engine that scans public fandom signals — social posts, streaming data, fan art, community participation, event attendance, and more. It uses machine learning to distinguish genuine fan passion from bots and artificial activity. Your R.E.C. Score is a cumulative, verifiable measure of your fandom impact. See our full documentation for the technical details.
What blockchains does LiveRecord support? +
LiveRecord is built natively on Solana. We use Solana mainnet-beta for production and devnet for testing. Solana's high throughput and low fees make it the ideal home for the fandom economy. Check our documentation for details on our deployment.
How can I start earning $REC? +
Connect your Solana wallet to the LiveRecord app. Your fandom activity will be automatically detected and scored by the R.E.C. Engine. The more genuine your engagement with supported IPs — idols, anime, games, music — the higher your R.E.C. Score and the more $REC you earn. Launch is live now at kickstart.easya.io.
Can IP owners and developers build on LiveRecord? +
Yes — LiveRecord is fully permissionless. Any IP owner, idol agency, music label, anime studio, or developer can integrate with the protocol without asking permission. Read our Developer Documentation to get started, or contact us at partnership@liverecord.fun for a guided partnership discussion.
