What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They serve various functions, such as remembering your preferences, keeping you logged in, and helping website owners understand how visitors use their site.

Similar technologies, such as local storage and session storage, work in comparable ways and are covered by this policy.

05

Browser-Specific Instructions

To manage cookies directly in your browser:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

For mobile devices, cookie management is typically found in your browser app settings under "Privacy" or "Site settings".
