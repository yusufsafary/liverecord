Community Guidelines

HOW WE
SHOW UP

01
Genuine Only

LiveRecord is built on authentic fandom. We celebrate real fan passion. Bots, coordinated manipulation, and inauthentic behavior are actively filtered by R.E.C. and unwelcome in the community.

02
Fans First

Every decision we make asks one question: is this better for fans? We're building for the people who keep entertainment alive — not the industry gatekeepers. Fan voice is the most important voice here.

03
Respect & Support

Our community spans every fandom, nationality, and background. We treat every fan and every IP with respect. Harassment, hate, and gatekeeping have no place here. Build each other up.

Our Belief

WHY WE
BUILD

Fans don't just consume culture — they create it. LiveRecord exists to make sure that creation never goes unrecognized again.

◎

Permissionless Access

Any fan, any IP, any community — LiveRecord is open to everyone. No gatekeepers. No whitelists. Connect your wallet and start building your record.

∞

Permanent Records

Your fandom history is permanent and yours. Once recorded onchain, no one can erase your contribution to the culture you love. It belongs to you forever.

◈

Community Governed

LiveRecord will be governed by $REC holders — the fans themselves. Protocol decisions, IP additions, reward allocations — the community decides, not the team.

✦

Transparent & Verifiable

All scores, all distributions, all decisions — published onchain and verifiable by anyone. No black boxes. No hidden allocations. Just code and community.
