How the epoch works

LiveRecord runs in 24-hour epochs. At the end of each epoch, the R.E.C. Engine finalizes scores for all active fans, publishes a Merkle root onchain, and opens the claim window. Your share of each epoch's distribution is proportional to your R.E.C. Score relative to all fans.

01

Connect Wallet

Link any Solana wallet. No KYC. No gatekeeping.

02

Link Fandom

Authorize R.E.C. Engine to observe your public fan activity — read-only.

03

Score Builds Daily

Your R.E.C. Score grows with every genuine fan action across all tracked IPs.

04

Claim $REC

At epoch end, claim your $REC rewards. Fast and low-cost on Solana.

Questions

$REC FAQ

When is the $REC token launch (TGE)?+
The Token Generation Event (TGE) date will be announced via our official @liverecordfun X account and our community channels. Follow us to be first to know. The public launch allocation (3% / 30M $REC) will be immediately liquid at TGE.
Where can I buy $REC?+
$REC will be available through the public launch event at kickstart.easya.io first. DEX listings and broader availability will follow. Until then, the best way to acquire $REC is by earning it through your fandom activity. Follow @liverecordfun for exchange listing announcements.
Is there a maximum supply?+
Yes. $REC has a fixed maximum supply of 1,000,000,000 (1 billion) tokens. There is no inflation after all fan reward epochs complete (approximately 10 years). The supply schedule is fixed in the smart contract.
What blockchain is $REC on?+
$REC is deployed as an SPL Token on Solana. Solana mainnet-beta powers governance, staking, fan rewards, and low-cost claims, all on a single fast network. See our documentation for details.
How do I use $REC in governance?+
Once the DAO launches (Phase 3, Q4 2025), any wallet holding $REC can participate in governance votes. You can also delegate your voting power to another wallet. Proposals require 100,000 $REC to submit and 4% quorum to pass. Read more in the governance documentation.
